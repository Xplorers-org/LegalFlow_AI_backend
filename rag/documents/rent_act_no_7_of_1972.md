# RENT ACT, NO. 7 OF 1972 (COMMERCIAL PROVISIONS & AMENDMENTS)
## DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA

### SECTION 20: REGULATION OF RENT PAYMENTS
1. No landlord shall receive or recover rent in excess of the authorized rent for commercial premises subject to the Rent Act.
2. Rent shall be payable on or before the due date specified in the written agreement, or in the absence of an agreement, by the tenth (10th) day of each calendar month.

### SECTION 21: DEFAULT IN PAYMENT OF RENT
1. A tenant shall be deemed to be in default of rent if rent due for any month has not been paid within one month of the due date.
2. Where a tenant is in default of rent for one month or more, the landlord may terminate the tenancy by giving notice in writing.

### SECTION 22: RESTRICTIONS ON EVICTION
No action or proceedings for the ejectment of the tenant of any premises shall be instituted except on the ground that:
(a) Rent has been in arrears for one month after it has become due;
(b) The premises are reasonably required for the occupation or business of the landlord;
(c) The tenant has caused substantial damage to the premises.
