# RECOVERY OF POSSESSION OF PREMISES GIVEN ON LEASE ACT, NO. 1 OF 2023
## PARLIAMENT OF THE DEMOCRATIC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF SRI LANKA

### SECTION 1: SHORT TITLE & COMMENCEMENT
This Act may be cited as the Recovery of Possession of Premises Given on Lease Act, No. 1 of 2023.

### SECTION 2: APPLICATION OF THE ACT
This Act shall apply to all written lease agreements entered into in respect of residential, commercial, or industrial premises in Sri Lanka, where the lessor seeks recovery of possession upon expiration or termination of the lease agreement.

### SECTION 3: SERVICE OF STATUTORY NOTICE TO QUIT
1. Where a lessee fails to hand over vacant possession of the leased premises upon expiration of the lease term, or where a lessee defaults on rent payments for more than fourteen (14) days after due date, the lessor may serve a written Notice to Quit.
2. The Notice to Quit shall give the lessee a period of not less than thirty (30) days from the date of receipt to vacate the premises and pay all outstanding arrears.
3. Service of notice shall be effected by registered post or personal service at the demised premises.

### SECTION 4: INSTITUTION OF SUMMARY LEGAL PROCEEDINGS
1. If the lessee fails to vacate the premises upon expiration of the thirty (30) day notice period specified in Section 3, the lessor may institute summary recovery proceedings in the District Court having jurisdiction.
2. The petition shall be accompanied by an affidavit stating:
   (a) The execution of the valid written lease agreement;
   (b) The default in payment or expiration of lease period;
   (c) Proof of service of the statutory Notice to Quit.

### SECTION 5: DECREE NISI & IMMEDIATE POSSESSION
1. Upon satisfaction of the documents presented under Section 4, the District Court shall issue a Decree Nisi requiring the lessee to show cause within fourteen (14) days why vacant possession should not be delivered to the lessor.
2. If the lessee fails to show cause or deposit the outstanding arrears in Court within fourteen (14) days, the Decree Nisi shall automatically be made absolute, and a Writ of Execution shall issue forthwith.

### SECTION 6: STATUTORY INTEREST & ARREARS RECOVERY
The Court shall, in addition to ordering recovery of possession, order the lessee to pay all outstanding arrears of rent together with statutory interest at the rate of twelve per centum (12%) per annum from the date of default until full payment.
